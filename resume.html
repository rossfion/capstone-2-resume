Profile

Why hire me as a Full Stack Web Developer? I have freelanced for ten years in a variety of fields, and taught Business Management, Information Technology (IT) and Media/Multimedia Technology for more than 12 years. In addition to this, I have been building websites since 2005 using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript from scratch as well as with frameworks. Furthermore, I have spent the last nine years maintaining a custom-built content management system with a front-end to support the work of the late African-American artist Morgan Monceaux.

After teaching IT and running my own business for over fifteen years, I finally got an opportunity to spend a lot more time creating and developing my own portfolio while educating myself to stay relevant as well as formalizing my experience to date.

At the moment, this is being achieved through completing the Web Developer 2023 Bootcamp Udemy course by Dr. Angela Yu of the London App Brewery, UK.

With my skills and experience, I am sure I can contribute to the success of any company and welcome any opportunities to show how I can help you reach your goals.

Authorized to work in the USA for any employer (Permanent Resident status).

100% remote work strongly preferred.
